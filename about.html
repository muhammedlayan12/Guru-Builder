About Us - Guru Builders & Developer

Founded in 2021 by visionary entrepreneur, Guru Builders & Developers is reshaping Pakistan’s real estate industry with innovative projects that reflect a perfect blend of entrepreneurship, quality, and luxury. Our commitment to excellence has enabled us to deliver a diverse range of residential and commercial developments, making a significant impact, particularly in Karachi.

Our flagship project, Al Mirha Galaxy Mobile Mall & Residency, exemplifies our entrepreneurial spirit and vision for creating modern, sustainable spaces. With meticulous attention to detail and an emphasis on community, we are setting new benchmarks in urban development, providing not just spaces but unique experiences.

We are also proud to introduce T20 Tower, Islamabad, Pakistan’s first cricket-themed high-rise offering 7-star amenities. This groundbreaking project brings together luxury living and a passion for sports, delivering an unmatched lifestyle.

At Guru Builders, we are not just builders; we are entrepreneurs shaping the future of real estate, crafting communities that stand the test of time and inspire generations to come.
